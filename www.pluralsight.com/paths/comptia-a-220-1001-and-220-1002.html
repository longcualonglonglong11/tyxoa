Authors: Glenn Weadock, Vlad Catrinescu, Daniel Lachance

The CompTIA A+ certification is immensely popular and often essential to many entry-level positions in the IT world. The 220-1001 and 220-1002 exams are updated versions of the 220-901 and 220-902 exams.
